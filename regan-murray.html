Regan Murray

ALUMNI

Regan is an undergraduate student studying Cartography and Geographic Information Systems at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. She joined the Gibbs lab in the fall of 2019 to conduct a review of existing data and literature on pasture and grazing land in the United States, with the ultimate goal of creating a comprehensive map. In her free time, Regan enjoys rock climbing with the Hoofer Mountaineering Club and cross country skiing.
